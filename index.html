Creative Coding: The Nature of Code Sketches

Explore my interactive sketches inspired by Daniel Shiffman's The Nature of Code! Every sketch is based on the p5.js JavaScript library, and most of them are designed to fit a 640x240 pixels frame :)

Sketches

  • Paint Splatter
    A generative paint splatter effect using Gaussian random distribution (both for color and position).
  • Perlin Noise Walker
    A particle that moves smoothly using Perlin noise.
  • Arrow Guided Acceleration
    A ball with linear movement whose acceleration is guided by up-arrow and down-arrow keys.
  • Bouncing Sphere
    A sphere that bounces within the canvas boundaries.
  • Gravitational Attraction Approach
    A simulation of gravitational attraction between a ball and the mouse.
